Who We Are

Sriram Groups is a convergence of global entrepreneurial vision, time-honoured values and the best business acumen. Sriram Groups is the brain-child of a group of like-minded stakeholders who bring with them over 35 years of management expertise.

Sriram Groups commenced operations in 1988 and in just over 35 years of its existence, has established a successful presence across a portfolio of business verticals.

Sriram Group's hallmark has been a focused Service-Centric apporch underscored by fair business practices and a commitment to superior standards.


Our Story

Started as a small financial office in 1988, our company took its first step in TNEB Contract work. With service mind we grow our service in PWD work on 2000. Commencing successful project, We grow a branch in NH & H/D contract work.

On further development we opened our first private business in IOCL Dealership (Sriram Agencis) on 2006. Having service as our commitment to develop a good community we founded Sriram Institute of Education on 2012.
